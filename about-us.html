About Us

A privately owned Information Technology staffing, consulting and talent management firm based in Cleveland, Ohio.

Our 20 years of combined staffing excellence and precise recruiting within Information Technology allow us the ability to offer IT Staffing Services, Flexible Consulting Solutions and overall industry leading Talent Management. Our office is focused in the Northeast Ohio, Akron/Canton and Columbus marketplaces with the ability to service clients across the US. HireTech prides itself on acting as a complete solutions provider for our clients within all areas Information Technology staff augmentation including full time/permanent staffing, consulting/project staffing and contract to hire solutions.

Staffing Excellence and Precise Recruiting

HireTech offers an honest and hard-working approach to solve your IT staffing needs. The combination of our hands on experience in the industry along with true understanding of enterprise information technology helps us find the best people and assist our client in reaching their goals faster and more cost effective. We strive to listen to our candidates career goals and long term expectations. We are skilled in knowing where the best professionals are in the city, what makes an awesome work place and who is hiring and what they are hiring. We feel this combination between servicing our client and candidate allows us to offer excellent advice to build winning teams, attract top notch talent and grow your business long term! We focus on diversity and offering equal opportunity to everyone across all gender, race and ethnic groups.

We Listen

Our Pledge to our clients and candidates is that "We Listen". We do our fair share of talking but HireTech will always listen to our client needs and candidates goals. We offer a service and part of that service is to be fair and honest! We listen and act in line with what the needs of our clients best interest and the long term career goals for our candidates. This ensures our services will always deliver a solid ROI, create high level solutions, solve complex IT problems or simply find a candidate that next great position in their career.
