Our Client/Hiring Process

HireTech Group will stand by our promise to listen and provide the highest level of customer service. You will see from our initial meetings to the final result of landing that great new consultant or full time staff member, HireTech Group will be there to ensure quality and satisfaction for our clients. We make a promise to stay out of the way but not forget our role in the staffing process!

Hiring Process Steps

  1. Job Order taken from the client

  2. Organize search perimeter to target the strongest possible matches for this client need.

  3. All candidates will be met in person, screened technically with specific questions or technical tests geared toward that skill set. In majority of cases, all references will be checked and qualified with either peer level or manager level recommendation.

  4. HireTech Group will then submit a small pool of resumes that best match the search criteria, follow up to ensure this is a viable list of options.

  5. The client can decide to interview any of the viable options and HireTech Group will coordinate all scheduling and accommodations for the face to face interview.

  6. We will ensure that all last minute candidate and client prep is taken care of before any face to face interview. This includes making sure the candidate has a list of all meeting attendees, parking info, client addresses, and contact information in order for the process to go smoothly.

  7. We then seek feedback from the client and candidate asap. If we have a match then we move to background checks/offers extended or 2ndinterview will be set up. If we don’t have a match, HireTech Group will continue sourcing more options and try to target a different candidate that might fit the overall need of this client.

  8. If we have a match, HireTech Group will perform all background checks and clear all reference checks necessary. Once all background checks and references are complete we will work with the client to extend an offer. Upon clients request we can help formulate formal offer letter and coordinate all start/first day of employment information

  9. Once the offer is formally accepted, HireTech Group will maintain constant contact to ensure the start of employment goes smoothly weather it’s a full time or consulting placement. If consulting, HireTech Group will make sure to gather information on who all needs to approve timecards. We will ensure all time tracking and hours reporting systems are understood and cleared for access.

  10. Maintain bi-weekly check-ups for the first month and then monthly for the duration of the assignment if consulting and over 6 months if a full time placement with our clients.

  11. Why pick HireTech Group over any other IT Staffing firm

  12. Personal network and qualified CRM database with over 10,000 top-notch IT Candidates in Cleveland, Akron/Canton and Columbus.

  13. IT knowledge and experienced offering staffing solutions that will eliminate wasted time searching and interviewing candidates that don’t fit the client’s needs.
