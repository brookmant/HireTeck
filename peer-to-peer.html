Peer to Peer Training

Peer to peer training is a concept we came up with to help candidates gain a little more knowledge while helping others out. It’s true person to person networking in its finest form.

Here it is

We connect our vast network of thousands and offer suggestions for technical training that could help catapult the candidate to a different level with technical skills.

The hardest part of interviewing is to not have the exact skill needed to get an offer or even noticed by some opportunities. We strive to help push people over the top technically. We help match skills and possible areas where IT Job Seekers can help other IT Job Seekers learn from each other. People help each other with the intention of learning new skills and helping another person grow technically.

Example #1

HireTech Group will connect a top-notch SQL Server Developer with excellent SSIS/SSAS skills and maybe lighter ASP.NET/C# Skills with a C#/ASP.NET superstar developer. The SQL Server developer can train the superstar in SSIS and SQL Server while at the same time learn key front end C#/ASP.NET skills from the other developer. They both learn valuable skills from each other. The industry is filled with many helpful people who love this idea and are willing to help!

Example #2

HireTech Group has a client looking to hire a SQL DBA with light skills in Oracle. Our SQL DBA candidate needs to learn a little more about Oracle. HireTech Group will connect an Oracle DBA with a SQL Server DBA to learn the ins and outs of how the databases differ. They will teach each other the in depth skills on how each database operates differently whether in a Windows environment or running in a Unix/Linux operating environment.

Begin Today

This is how Peer to Peer training works. We will gladly arrange a quick training/knowledge sharing session for you if you desire. We always look for active volunteers who might be willing to participate. Please don’t hesitate to contact us if you your looking to help train someone or if you in need of sharping your skills in certain areas.
