Welcome to HireTechGroup

Welcome to HireTechGroup.com! Thanks for visiting our site. Based in Cleveland, Ohio we’ve been in the IT staffing industry for 20+ years.

Clients

Visit our client area. You will find everything you need to get to know HireTech Group better.

What to Expect

A unique blend of sales and IT recruiting experience that helps us truly understand the technical/business problem and build true partnerships with our customers.

Areas of Focus

We assist clients across all vertical business markets from the largest Fortune 500 companies, privately held corporations, major mid market companies as well as start ups supported by Venture Capital and Angel Investors.

Our Services

We offer a hands on approach that helps clients understand the job market conditions and talent pools within the NorthEast Ohio IT community. We know what skills are the hottest and moving quickest at any given time. We know who is hiring and what they need to find those top notch employees. We offer flexible payment terms, creative invoicing and other billing solutions that won’t get in the way of doing businss at a fair price!

Job Seekers

We offer straight forward industry leading advice for ways to land the job and grow your technical career. We are true down to earth professionals that help job seekers see the bright side of IT staffing.

Current Openings

Please review our current openings frequently to find a position that best suits your skills and abilities. We recieve new openings on a daily basis so don’t forget to check in with us here at HireTech Group!

Submit a Resume

Our resume submission is quick and easy. Please click on the link and begin. One of our recruiting professionals will contact you to set a time to discuss your skill set and meet in person.

Our Process

HireTech Group will never charge a fee to our candidates for any of our staffing services including general resume writing, technical skills assessment or peer to peer training.
